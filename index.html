Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our premium sherry barrels and services.

DO certified casks are brand new barrels that have been seasoned for 12 months with authentic sherry and certified by the Xeres-Jerez-Sherry regulatory body. Historic solera casks are ex-sherry barrels from centuries-old solera systems, offering deeper character from years of sherry aging. Both provide authentic Spanish provenance, but historic casks offer more complex, mature flavors while certified casks provide consistent, controlled seasoning.

The choice depends on your desired flavor profile and base spirit. Fino and Manzanilla casks work well with lighter spirits and beers, adding mineral and saline notes. Amontillado provides balanced complexity, while Oloroso offers rich, robust flavors perfect for whisky and dark beers. Pedro Xim√©nez creates sweet, dessert-like profiles.
